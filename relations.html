04 / Relations

WITH OTHERS

Iribiopolis is structured as a practice of collaboration, not a closed authorship.

COMMUNITIES

Local knowledge

People living with damaged, abandoned or changing landscapes hold knowledge that no survey can replace. Walking, conversation and shared interpretation begin the work.

INSTITUTIONS

Public capacity

We work with environmental, heritage, educational and civic organisations that can support long-term access, care, research and public use.

COLLABORATORS

Plural expertise

Ecologists, artists, historians, fabricators, researchers, engineers and local practitioners expand what a landscape proposal can know and do.

WHAT WE DO

Research made spatial.

Site recording, archival and GIS mapping, environmental observation, public engagement, spatial strategy, design development, exhibitions, workshops, prototypes and publications.

WORK WITH US

Bring a site that resists a clean solution.

Iribiopolis is interested in landscapes affected by abandonment, extraction, flooding, contested heritage, ecological change, displacement or incomplete redevelopment.
