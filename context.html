POSITION

Against the clean ruin.

Iribiopolis rejects the ruin as a picturesque image emptied of conflict. Ruins are active environments shaped by property, access, ecological succession, memory, violence, maintenance and neglect.

DESIGN CULTURE

Atmosphere must carry an argument.

The visual language of the practice draws from weathered interfaces, archaeological fragments, field instruments, game environments and speculative landscapes. Every effect supports the central idea: ground is unstable, time is visible and completion is never absolute.
